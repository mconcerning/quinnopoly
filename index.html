Quinnopoly! is a two – five player board game designed to be enjoyed by trillions around the globe. The average game time is between 2 and 10 minutes. Inspired by both Monopoly and The Game of Life, Quinnopoly! isn’t a standard game about a race to retirement or a race to… get a monopoly. Instead, Quinnopoly! is an original game with never-before-seen mechanics. It is enjoyable for the whole family and is relatable to all cultures with its classic themes of not going broke and dying.
